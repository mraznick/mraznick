About Me

NYC-Based Career Salesman Gone Coder

Originally from Omaha, Nebraska, I had moved to New York City in August 2015 to pursue a career in film, media, and live music. After some great projects over the course of 4 months or so, the freelance life turned out to be not-so-sustainable for me. As a result, I chose to go into tech sales for a steadier income.

Despite my intial plan of working in sales short-term, I ended up finding a knack for it, and worked my way up the ladder over the course of about 6 years. I eventually landed a Sales Director position at an emerging tech company, working larger deals with better income. But at that point, I realized I was burning out, and remembered I never really intended my career to be in sales.
